# Privacy Policy for MathBridge

Last updated: March 04, 2026

## Overview

MathBridge ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and disclosure practices. By using the MathBridge Chrome Extension, you agree to the terms of this policy.

## Data Collection and Usage

MathBridge operates entirely locally on your device. We **do not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage metrics, or browsing history.

1.  **Clipboard Access**: The extension requires `clipboardWrite` permission strictly to format and place the mathematical formulas you explicitly choose to copy into your system clipboard. We do not read your clipboard history.
2.  **Local Storage**: The extension uses `chrome.storage.sync` strictly to save your preferred formula copy format (e.g., MathML, Plain Text, PNG). This preference remains on your device or is synced securely via your Google Chrome account. We have no access to this data.
3.  **Host Permissions**: The extension requests access to specific AI chat platforms (e.g., Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude) solely to detect and extract the LaTeX source code of the math formulas rendered on those pages. No chat data or other content is ever read, stored, or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

MathBridge does not integrate with any third-party analytics trackers or advertising networks. 

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our GitHub repository. Keep an eye on our [Changelog](CHANGELOG.md) for related updates.
